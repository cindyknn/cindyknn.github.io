Hi, I'm Cindy Nguyen!

B.S. Electrical & Computer Engineering student at Rice University

My name is Cindy Nguyen, and I am currently an undergraduate majoring in Electrical & Computer Engineering at Rice University.

I am a researcher at the Rice Parallel Hardware Applications research lab in collaboration with the Texas Medical Center. I am also involved in many engineering extracurriculars, such as Rice Wind Energy and Rice Eclipse Rocketry. In my own time, I love working on personal projects as laid out in this portfolio.

This website is not only my personal engineering portfolio but it also serves a greater purpose about which I am very passionate: it is home to STEMess, a blog I founded in 2020 to help promote and encourage Vietnamese female students to pursue STEM fields on my journey as a fellow woman in STEM (navigate to STEMess page).
